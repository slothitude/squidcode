# Default Style Guide

## General Principles

- Use clear, concise language
- Prefer active voice over passive voice
- Keep sentences under 25 words when possible
- Use simple words when complex ones add no meaning
- Break long paragraphs into shorter ones

## Clarity

- Avoid jargon unless the audience expects it
- Define acronyms on first use
- Use concrete examples instead of abstract descriptions
- Replace ambiguous pronouns with specific nouns

## Structure

- Lead with the most important information
- Use descriptive headings
- One idea per paragraph
- Use bullet points for lists of three or more items

## Tone

- Professional but approachable
- Direct without being rude
- Helpful and informative
- Avoid hedging language like "perhaps" or "maybe" when certainty is appropriate
